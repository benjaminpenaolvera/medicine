Our Mission and Values.

Our mission is to transform your medical needs into a pleasurable and one of a kind experience by managing the logistics of your medical trip and offering quality services with touristic options to complement your experience in Mexico.

By choosing Medtrip to arrange your medical trip, you are choosing comfort and security.

Our Core Values:

Quality

Our Medical and Tourism Service Providers are highly qualified medical providers with international recognition are ready to take care of your medical needs. These standards validate the quality of the equipment and techniques used in each establishment, thus allowing you to have peace of mind when choosing to arrange your medical trip with Medtrip.

Confidentiality

All the personal information you provide, including your medical history and diagnosis, is treated as delicate information. The Medtrip Customer Platform was created and is protected by maximum level security to ensure the safety of your private information.

Commitment

We are committed to putting your health and safety above anything else and providing a one of a kind, pleasurable, and unforgettable experience in Mexico.

Integrity

At Medtrip, we stand by our mission and always have a Call Center Specialist available to provide assistance. Our Medtrip Customer Platform was created for you to be able to access all of your information directly from your computer, tablet, or mobile device at any time.

Innovation

In order to progress and adapt to your needs, we are constantly promoting innovation within Medtrip by supporting medical research, foundations, institutions, and the specialization of all our employees. We hold our Service Providers at high standards, constantly promoting national and international certifications and accreditations.
